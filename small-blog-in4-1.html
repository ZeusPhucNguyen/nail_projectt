8 Ways To Properly Care For Your Nails ( Part 1 )

Direct contact with chemicals causes nails to become stained and severely damaged. Hours in beauty salons are not making the situation any better. Follow these tips on how to take care of your nails and you'll be extremely happy with their amazing results.

1- Soak your hands in hot olive oil

Olive oil provides moisture to the body, especially on sensitive areas: skin, hair and nails. Natural oils work to prevent breaks occurring on the nail due to physical and chemical effects. Soaking and warming with olive oil twice a month is the minimum nail care you should apply.

2-Apply lotion to the cuticles

The epidermis is the most difficult part of skin to care for and handle when damaged. Infection occurs if the wounds are not properly cared for. Apply lotion around your nails every night before going to bed. That way, it's much easier to remove or re-glue the cuticles. It won't happen again if you incorporate vitamin E in nail care

3-Wear gloves when doing housework

Using gloves when doing chores is an effective way to protect nails from the impact of the environment. The chemicals in floor cleaning solutions and bowls are extremely harmful to the nails and surrounding skin. Garden soil and drilled well water corrode and yellow your nails when you are exposed to them too often.

4-Eat foods rich in biotin

Biotin works to enhance quality and vitality for damaged nails. Add this active ingredient as soon as you detect symptoms of damage such as: brittle, sensitive, slow growing on the nail. Incorporate liver, cooked eggs, whole grains, cauliflower and butter in your daily diet if you want effective nail care.
