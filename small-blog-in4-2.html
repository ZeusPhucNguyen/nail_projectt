8 Ways To Properly Care For Your Nails ( Part 2 )

Direct contact with chemicals causes nails to become stained and severely damaged. Hours in beauty salons are not making the situation any better. Follow these tips on how to take care of your nails and you'll be extremely happy with their amazing results.

5-Supplement with multivitamins

Vitamins are active ingredients that make up the beauty and vitality of the whole body. Nails become dry and brittle if they do not receive vitamins A and E in time. A diet rich in protein and calcium is essential if you want to limit the effects of nail polish.

6-Do not use acetone nail polish remover

TUse up to 2 nail polishes per month if you don't want to deal with chemical damage. Acetone is also contraindicated when you have thin and sensitive nails. Replace alcohol and water when you want to remove old nail polish. It is a bit time-consuming, but they are effective and safe to apply.

7-Massage your nails regularly

This stimulates blood flow and helps nails grow faster. Gently massage cuticles and fingertips with hot lotion or oil. Applying moisturizer after washing hands can also help improve nail strength.

8-Drink water actively

Drinking plenty of water is an effective way to nourish and care for your nails. Dry and discoloration occurs when nails become dehydrated and lack moisture. Soaking and relaxing with rose water after bathing is a pleasant experience when you're tired.
